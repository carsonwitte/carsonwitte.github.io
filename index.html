Carson R. Witte

I am a PhD student at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory of Columbia University, studying air-sea interaction – and its modulation by surface phenomena such as sea ice, microbiology, and precipitation – with Dr. Christopher Zappa. My research interests, broadly speaking, lie in connecting direct observations at small scales to processes occurring across wide spatiotemporal domains.
